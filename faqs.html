Frequently Asked Questions

What is a patient portal?

A patient portal is a secure online website that gives patients convenient 24-hour access to personal health information from anywhere with an Internet connection. Users can view health information, schedule appointments, and communicate with their healthcare team.

How is this different from MyChart?

Our patient portal offers a similar comprehensive and user-friendly experience to MyChart, providing features like secure messaging, appointment scheduling, and access to test results. While MyChart is a specific product by Epic, our solution is designed to integrate with various EHR systems and offer a customizable experience for your organization.

Can patients access their records via a mobile app?

Yes, our patient portal is fully responsive and can be accessed via any web browser on desktop or mobile devices. We also offer a dedicated MyChart app-like experience for enhanced mobile accessibility, allowing for easy access and management on the go.

Is the patient portal secure?

Absolutely. We prioritize patient data security and privacy. Our patient portal is built with robust encryption, strict access controls, and adheres to all relevant healthcare data protection regulations, including HIPAA.

What features are included?

Our patient portal includes features such as online appointment scheduling, secure messaging with providers, access to lab results and medical history, medication refill requests, and online bill pay. It's designed to offer a complete suite of tools for patient engagement, much like a comprehensive MyChart login experience.
